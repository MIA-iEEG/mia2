## Dataset Paradigm

The examples shown in the MIA tutorials are based on a picture-naming task:

- 15 participants with epilepsy under presurgical evaluation with SEEG.
- 108 objects depicted as black-on-white line drawings.
- Data acquired at 1000 Hz with a 256-channel BrainAmp amplifier system.
- A scalp electrode placed at Fz used as the recording reference.
- Full paradigm description in [Dubarry et al. 2017](https://doi.org/10.1177/0956797616681296).
